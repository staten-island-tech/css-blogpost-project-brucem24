The Echidna

An Echidna may look like a normal animal to many people. The Echidna is an egg-laying mammal, called "Monotremes", which are extremely rare species in fact. It is one out of five monotremes in the entire world, which include four Echidna species and one Platypus. Click the link to find out more about this fascinating critter. The Echidna

The Pangolin

The Pangolin may look like a reptile to most eyes but it is actually a scaled mammal! The pangolin is the most trafficked animal in the world because of their meat and scales.
